The story

of The Lifedu

Founded in 2021 by Yoshitha Challapalli, Lifedu was designed to make a positive impact on the education system. The Lifedu offers customized solutions powered by AI to meet the needs of students' mental health in each school.

We hope to be that friend for students that teaches, empowers, and gets them through their school life.

We are The Lifedu.

We are on a mission to make education impactful.

Yoshitha Challapalli

Chief Executive Officer

I don't want any child to experience what I went through when I was in school. With very little awareness and attention paid towards mental health, I suffered from severe anxiety through-out my high school experience. This led me to perform poorly in my classes and I almost failed my board exams.

I never understood my true potential until I found ways to cope with anxiety. I wish schools took that one small extra step to just show their students they care, it makes a whole lot of difference.

With Lifedu, I hope we can be the ones to teach students things that aren't traditionally taught and be their friend through school life.
